Famous Personality's

Hades

Hades is both the ancient Greek god of the underworld and the name of the underworld itself. He is responsible for governing the realm of the dead and is often depicted as a stern and mysterious figure in Greek mythology.

Persephone

Persephone is a prominent figure in Greek mythology, known as the daughter of Demeter and Zeus. Her abduction by Hades and eventual return from the underworld explains the origin of seasons.

Charon

Charon is a figure in Greek mythology, the ferryman who transports souls of the deceased across the river Styx to the underworld. He's often depicted as a grim and silent figure.

Cerberus

Cerberus is a multi-headed dog guardian of the underworld in Greek mythology, preventing souls from escaping and living beings from entering. His portrayal symbolizes the boundary between life and death.
